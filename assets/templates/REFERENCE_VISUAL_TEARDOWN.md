## Subject and hierarchy

Describe every visible subject, object, copy block, priority, and identity-bearing detail.

## Style, color, and lighting

Record art technique, palette roles, gradients, light direction, bloom, shadows, reflections, and atmosphere.

## Composition and materials

Record source-space sections, alignment axes, margins, overlaps, fixed layers, depth, and material treatment.

## Typography and fine detail

Record exact copy, font evidence, size, weight, tracking, line height, icons, texture, particles, strokes, radii, and one-pixel edges.

## Interaction and motion evidence

Record hit targets, states, scroll ownership, triggers, timing, and unknowns that cannot be proven from the reference.

For every possible continuation, record its status in `REFERENCE_COVERAGE.json`. A cropped list, card, or asset is `partial` until additional source evidence proves the hidden part.
